About Us

BandMatch is an online community based platform for local musicians to connect with each other and have a unique opportunity to meet up and possibly create a successful band. This website is all about sharing and caring about fellow musicians and if possible (and encouraged), meet up and have jam sessions wherever convenient and build new trust and relationships.

Why You Need it

  1. Gives you a space to create and update your musical portfolio.

  2. It gives you the option to search for musicians by sorting out categories.

  3. It is safe and inspirational.
